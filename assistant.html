AI Health Assistant

What Can CareBot Do?

  • Answer health questions 24/7
  • Provide first-aid steps for common emergencies
  • Offer personalized prevention tips
  • Guide you to reliable resources and images

Example Interactions

  • User: What should I do for a burn?
  • CareBot: Cool the burn under running water for 10 minutes. Do not apply ice. Cover with a clean cloth.

Best Practices

  • Always verify advice with a healthcare professional.
  • Use the assistant for guidance, not diagnosis.
  • In emergencies, call local authorities.
