Realtime Dehydration Monitoring

How It Works

The system uses real-time sensor data to monitor your hydration status. Animated progress bars and color-coded alerts help you stay aware of your risk level. When dehydration is detected, you receive instant notifications and actionable advice.

Visual Guide

  • Blue: Well hydrated
  • Yellow: Mild dehydration
  • Red: High risk – drink water immediately

Tips for Staying Hydrated

  • Drink water regularly, not just when thirsty.
  • Monitor urine color – pale is best.
  • Increase intake during hot weather or exercise.
