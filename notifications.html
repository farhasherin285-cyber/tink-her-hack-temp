Notifications & Smart Alerts

How Notifications Work

Our system sends notifications only when your health is at risk. Alerts are prioritized by severity and can escalate to authorities if needed.

Visual Scenarios

  • Yellow: Mild risk – self-care recommended
  • Orange: Moderate risk – take action soon
  • Red: High risk – emergency response, authorities notified

  • Visual logs for compliance checks
